Anthropic's Mythos 5 export ban triggers geopolitical crisis

The US government has blocked foreign access to Anthropic's latest model, prompting senior Anthropic technical staff to fly to Washington to negotiate directly with White House officials; both sides say they want a resolution but none has been reached. For AI PMs in regulated corporations — especially those outside the US — this is a forcing function: any product roadmap built on a single frontier model provider now carries geopolitical supply-chain risk that must be surfaced to leadership and mitigated through multi-model or open-weight fallback strategies
